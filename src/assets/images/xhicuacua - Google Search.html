      Chihuahua
      Dog breed

      Description

      The Chihuahua is the smallest breed of dog and is named after the state of Chihuahua in Mexico. Chihuahuas come in a wide variety of colors, and two coat lengths. Wikipedia
      Hypoallergenic: No
      Life expectancy: 12 – 20 years
      Origin: Mexico
      Mass: 1.5 – 3 kg
      Temperament: Lively, Devoted, Alert, Quick, Courageous
      Did you know: Chihuahua is the fourth-most popular dog breed by registrations (107,114) worldwide. wikipedia.org
